What I Offer

Each engagement is shaped by context, community, and the specific terrain of transformation you are navigating.

01
Consulting

Strategic advisory services for faith communities, educational institutions, nonprofits, and corporate clients. Engagements are tailored: from single-session strategy sessions to long-term advisory relationships.

  • Organizational strategy & visioning
  • Leadership coaching & development
  • Justice-centered institutional design
  • Program development & evaluation

Individual · Organizational · Executive

02
Facilitation & Training

Transformative facilitation grounded in justice-centered pedagogy, liberation theology, and community organizing. Available as half-day workshops, full-day immersives, multi-day retreats, or ongoing cohort experiences.

  • Race, gender, and power in organizations
  • Beloved Community leadership formation
  • Conflict transformation & restorative practice
  • Strategic planning & visioning retreats
  • Cross-movement organizing

Workshop · Immersive · Retreat · Cohort

03
Speaking & Preaching

Keynote addresses, sermons, lectures, and conference presentations at the convergence of theology, education, race, and liberation. A voice that moves from the particular to the universal — grounded in story, rigorous in analysis.

  • Conference keynotes & plenaries
  • Guest preaching & revival
  • University lectures & panels
  • Denominational convenings

Keynote · Preaching · Lecture · Panel

All engagements are available in-person, virtually, or in hybrid formats. National and international travel is available for select engagements.
